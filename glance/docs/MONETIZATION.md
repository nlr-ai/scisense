# GLANCE Premier Regard -- Monetization Strategy

## Context

GLANCE is the validation layer of SciSense's Visual Evidence Compiler (VEC). It proves -- quantitatively -- that a GA transfers its information hierarchy correctly. The business model turns this proof into revenue.

**Current state (March 2026):** Platform running at localhost. 47 GA images across 15 domains. Semantic scoring operational. Flux (stream) mode live. No external users yet.

---

## 4 Revenue Streams

### 1. VEC Service (consulting, high margin)

**What:** SciSense produces or audits Graphical Abstracts for journal publishers, pharma companies, and research institutions using the VEC methodology. GLANCE provides the quantitative proof that the deliverable works.

**Deliverable:** GA design + GLANCE validation report showing Taux_S9b, speed-accuracy profile, cross-profile quadrant analysis.

**Pricing:** EUR 850+/day (SciSense tarif plancher). Retainers at EUR 4000+/month.

**Available now:** VEC methodology exists. GLANCE runs locally. The workflow is: client provides data -> VEC generates GA -> GLANCE validates -> deliver GA + validation report.

**Timeline:** Revenue-ready now (pending first client).

### 1b. GLANCE GA Audit (entry-level product, EUR 99)

**What:** A one-shot audit of a single Graphical Abstract. The client submits their GA, and receives a scored analysis with exact, prioritized design recommendations. This is the entry point into the GLANCE ecosystem -- low friction, high perceived value.

**Deliverable:** The recommendation report generated by `recommender.py`:
- Overall score (0-1) based on graph tension analysis
- CRITICAL fixes: nodes making unvalidated claims (high weight, low stability)
- HIGH fixes: unresolved design elements (high energy)
- Channel upgrade paths with expected S9b improvement (e.g., "Replace bubble chart with bar chart: +20-30% comprehension")
- Accessibility warnings (colorblindness, text density, thumbnail readability)
- Strengths: what already works well in the design

**Why it works:** A score alone is worthless -- anyone can run a survey. The value is in the *specific fix* with the *expected impact*. "Your GA uses area encoding (Stevens beta 0.7). Switch to length encoding and S9b improves +20-30%. Here is exactly how." That precision is what makes EUR 99 feel like a bargain.

**Upsell path:** GA Audit (EUR 99) -> full GLANCE Certification (EUR 200-500) -> VEC redesign service (EUR 850+/day). The audit report creates demand for the redesign.

**Pricing:** EUR 99 per GA. No participant recruitment needed -- the audit is based on the information graph, not live testing. Instant delivery.

**Timeline:** Revenue-ready now. Requires: `recommender.py` (done), client-facing report template, payment integration.

### 2. GLANCE Certification (productized service)

**What:** A standardized certification that a GA has passed GLANCE validation. Publishers or pharma companies submit their GAs and receive a certification score.

**Deliverable:** GLANCE Certification Mark: "This GA achieved Taux_S9b >= 0.80 across N participants in GLANCE Premier Regard testing." Includes Taux_S9b, Score_S9c, speed-accuracy breakdown, profile quadrant analysis.

**Pricing model:** Per-GA certification fee. Tiered: basic (N=20, EUR 200-500), full (N=50+, EUR 500-1500), with cross-profile stratification.

**Timeline:** Q3 2026. Requires: deployed platform (not localhost), 50+ participant pool, standardized report template.

### 3. Platform (freemium SaaS)

**What:** GLANCE as a self-service web platform. Researchers upload their GAs, recruit participants, and get GLANCE scores.

**Free tier:** Upload 1 GA, run up to 10 tests, see basic Taux_S9b and GLANCE composite.

**Paid tier:** Multiple GAs, A/B testing (VEC vs control), full profile quadrant analysis, S10 saillance in stream mode, export raw data.

**Pricing model:** Subscription. EUR 50-200/month for researchers, EUR 500-2000/month for publishers.

**Timeline:** Q4 2026 - Q1 2027. Requires: cloud deployment, multi-tenant DB, user authentication, participant recruitment pipeline.

### 4. Data Licensing (long-term)

**What:** Aggregated, anonymized GLANCE data sold to publishers, visualization researchers, and design tool companies.

**Value proposition:** The GLANCE dataset is the only standardized, cross-domain, cross-profile comprehension benchmark for scientific visualizations. No equivalent exists.

**Products:**
- Annual benchmark report: "State of GA Comprehension 2027" (aggregated Taux_S9b by domain, chart type, encoding channel)
- Research dataset: anonymized test results for meta-analysis
- Design guidelines: empirically validated rules for GA design (derived from GLANCE data)

**Pricing model:** Report subscriptions (EUR 5000-20000/year), dataset licensing (per-query or annual), consulting on top.

**Timeline:** 2027+. Requires: 1000+ tests, 10+ domains, published validation paper.

## Killer Metrics for Sales

### Recommendation Report: The Explainability Edge

The recommendation engine (`recommender.py`) transforms abstract scores into concrete actions. This is the differentiator between "you scored 45%" and "here are 3 specific changes that will bring you to 80%, with expected impact for each."

**Sales pitch:** "Your GA scored 0.59 overall. We found 1 CRITICAL issue (your central claim has no empirical backing -- weight 1.0, stability 0.4), 3 HIGH issues (unresolved visual elements), and your area encoding compresses perceived differences by 30% (Stevens beta 0.7). Switch to length encoding: +20-30% comprehension. Here's the full report."

The report is generated by `recommender.py` against the GA's information graph. No participants needed -- this is a structural analysis. The recommendation report is the core deliverable of the EUR 99 GA Audit product and the entry point into the GLANCE sales funnel.

### Delta_spoiler: The VEC Differentiator

```
Delta_spoiler = S9b(title_only) - S9b(nude)
```

If Delta_spoiler is high (> +0.10), the GA's title is carrying the information -- the GA is decorative. If Delta_spoiler is low (< +0.10), the GA encodes information independently of text context.

**Sales pitch:** "Your current GA relies on the title to transfer information. With VEC, the GA itself carries the message. Here's the GLANCE proof: Delta_spoiler dropped from 0.35 to 0.05."

This metric is not yet implemented (requires `stimulus_condition` beyond `nude`), but it is the single most compelling data point for enterprise sales.

### Delta_S9b: The A/B Proof

```
Delta_S9b = Taux_S9b(VEC) - Taux_S9b(control)
```

**Sales pitch:** "Your area-encoded GA achieves 45% correct hierarchy perception. The VEC version achieves 85%. That's a +40 point improvement, statistically significant (McNemar chi2 = 12.3, p < 0.001). Your readers are 2x more likely to understand the key finding."

This metric is operational now. Every GA in the library has both a VEC bar chart and an area-encoded control.

### S10 x S9b: The Full Chain

**Sales pitch:** "Your GA doesn't just need to be understood -- it needs to be noticed. In our simulated scroll test, your GA was selected 78% of the time (scroll-stopping validated) AND achieved 82% correct hierarchy perception. The S10 x S9b chain metric is 0.64 -- above the 0.56 threshold for demonstrated clinical impact potential."
