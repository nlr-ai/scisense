## Market Context & Research Insights

### Medical Affairs Trends 2025
Based on research from [McKinsey](https://www.mckinsey.com/~/media/mckinsey/industries/life%20sciences/our%20insights/a%20vision%20for%20medical%20affairs%20in%202025/a-vision-for-medical-affairs-in-2025.pdf), [ZS](https://www.zs.com/content/dam/pdfs/ZS-medical-affairs-outlook-report-2025.pdf), and [Medical Affairs Specialist](https://medicalaffairsspecialist.org/blog/medical-science-liaison-trends-in-2025-a-strategic-evolution):

- **Real-World Evidence (RWE)** generation is critical for demonstrating value
- **Digital transformation** with AI-driven content and omnichannel engagement
- **Patient-centric communication** is a growing priority
- **Scientific communication challenges**: adapting to diverse stakeholder needs (HCPs, KOLs, DOLs, payers)
- **Standardization & compliance** across medical affairs teams

### Company-Specific Insights

**Sanofi Vaccines:**
- Goal: 10 new vaccine candidates in clinical trials by 2025
- Focus on mRNA vaccine thermostability challenges
- Recent acquisition of Nuvaxovid from Novavax ([source](https://ir.novavax.com/press-releases/2025-11-04-Novavax-Completes-U-S-Marketing-Authorization-Transfer-to-Sanofi-for-Nuvaxovid-TM-,-Triggering-a-25-Million-Milestone-Payment))
- Scientific communication as "voice of the company"

**Valneva:**
- IXCHIQ (Chikungunya vaccine) safety review for elderly populations ([source](https://valneva.com/press-release/valneva-provides-update-on-recommendation-for-use-of-its-chikungunya-vaccine-by-french-authorities/))
- Lyme disease vaccine VLA15 with Pfizer - Phase 3 data expected H1 2026
- €127M revenue in 9M 2025 - growing travel vaccines market

**Boiron:**
- Scientific Affairs teams managing evidence communication for homeopathy
- Cannabis médical project in development
- Need for rigorous scientific positioning
